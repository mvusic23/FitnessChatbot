## Facility rules

1. Wipe down equipment after use.
2. Re-rack weights and return accessories to designated areas.
3. Closed-toe athletic shoes required on the gym floor.
4. Personal training sessions must be booked at the front desk or via the member app at least 24 hours in advance.

## Equipment zones

- **Zone A**: Free weights and squat racks (4 racks).
- **Zone B**: Cable machines and functional training area.
- **Zone C**: Cardio (treadmills, rowers, bikes).
