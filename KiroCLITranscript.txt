## Kiro


## Što radi `MAX_HISTORY_TURNS`


`MAX_HISTORY_TURNS` kontrolira **koliko prošlih "turnova" razgovora chatbot pamti** u memoriji. Jedan "turn" = jedan par poruka (korisnikova poruka + odgovor asistenta).


Konkretno:


1. **Definicija** — u `config.py` se čita iz environment varijable (default: `20`).
2. **Korištenje** — u `main.py` se ta vrijednost prosljeđuje klasi `Conversation(max_turns=20)`.
3. **Efekt** — u `conversation.py`, metoda `_trim()` ograničava internu listu poruka na `max_turns * 2` poruka (jer svaki turn ima 2 poruke: user + assistant). Kad se preko
rači limit, starije poruke se brišu — ostaju samo zadnjih N turnova.


**Primjer:** Ako je `MAX_HISTORY_TURNS=20`, chatbot pamti zadnjih 20 pitanja i 20 odgovora (ukupno 40 poruka). Sve starije poruke se odbacuju. To sprječava da kontekst posl
an LLM-u postane prevelik (i prespor/preskup), a istovremeno omogućuje chatbotu da se referira na nedavni razgovor.

*   **What it is:** The Crab Walk is exactly what it sounds like – you move forward and backward in a crab-like fashion, using your hands and feet to propel yourself.
*   **What it works:** It's a full-body exercise, engaging your core, glutes, shoulders, chest, and legs. It’s also great for coordination and stability.
*   **How to do it:**
    1.  **Starting Position:** Sit on the floor with your knees bent and your feet flat. Place your hands behind you, fingers pointing forward, shoulder-width apart.
    2.  **Lift:** Engage your core and glutes to lift your hips off the floor. Your body should form an upside-down "V" shape.
    3.  **Movement:** Simultaneously push through your hands and feet to move forward a short distance.
    4.  **Return:** Bring your hands and feet back to the starting position.
    5.  **Repeat:** Continue alternating forward and backward movements.


**Important Considerations:**


*   **Core Engagement:** Keeping your core tight is crucial for stability and to prevent lower back strain.
*   **Small Steps:** Don't try to move too far with each step. Small, controlled movements are more effective and safer.
*   **Shoulder Health:** If you have any shoulder issues, be cautious and avoid pushing through pain.

## Kiro


Trebam promijeniti `retrieve_context` da vraća i score-ove, te `format_context` da ih uključi u ispis. Najjednostavnije je dodati distance u `format_context` output:


Sad moram provjeriti da `query` u `vector_store.py` zaista vraća `distance` u hitovima:


`distance` je već u hitovima. ChromaDB koristi cosine distance (0 = identičan, 1 = potpuno različit), pa `1 - distance` daje relevance score (1.0 = savršen pogodak, 0.0 = p
otpuno irelevantno).


Gotovo. Sad će RAG Context panel prikazivati relevance score za svaki chunk, npr:


```
[source: vzb.txt | relevance: 0.82]
76: CrabWalk, Instructions: ...
```


Score ide od 0.00 (potpuno irelevantno) do 1.00 (savršen pogodak).

## Kiro


Izgleda da se `format_context` ne koristi za prikaz u panelu — panel prikazuje nešto drugo. Pogledajmo main.py detaljnije da vidim odakle dolazi taj formatirani ispis:


Sad vidim — chunkovi su formatirani s "Exercise #..." ali `format_context` dodaje `[source: ... | relevance: ...]` header. Problem je da taj header se ne vidi u tvom outputu. Pogledajmo kako se panel prikazuje u main.py:


Panel prikazuje `rag_context` koji dolazi iz `retrieve_context` → `format_context`. Format bi trebao biti:
```
[source: vzb.txt | relevance: 0.85]
Exercise #1: Crunch
...
```


Ali u tvom outputu nema `[source: ...]` linije. To znači da se `format_context` ne koristi za panel, ili se koristi ali bez `[source:]` dijela. Provjerim — `[source: ...]` u rich konzoli se interpretira kao markup tag!


Problem je bio da Rich interpretira `[source: vzb.txt | relevance: 0.85]` kao markup tag i skriva ga. Escapao sam uglatu zagradu s `\[` pa će se sad prikazati kao tekst.


Svaki chunk u RAG Context panelu će sad imati header poput:


```
